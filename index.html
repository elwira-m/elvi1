Images

Why is it soimportant?

Images may be two-dimeinsional, such as a photograph, screen display, and as well as a three-dimensional, such as a statue or hologram.

Volatile image

Is one that exists only for a short period of time. This may be a reflection of an object by a mirror, a projection of a camera obscura, or a scene displayed on a cathode ray tube.

Mental image

Exists in an individual's mind, as something one remembers or imagines. The subject of an image need not be real; it may be an abstract concept, such as a graph, function, or "imaginary" entity.

Still image

A typical font may contain hundreds or even thousands of glyphs, often representing characters from many different languages.
